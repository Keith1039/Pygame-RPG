Various items that you find might have other uses.
In this menu, two items can be combined to create another item.
2 seemingly useless items can combine to create a key item that is required to continue on.
In order to beat this game be sure to alwaydies try to combine anything and everything.
Even if two items don't end up making anything, you won't lose them by trying so go wild.
Items are only consumed when a new item is succesfully created.
